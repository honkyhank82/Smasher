Child Safety Policy

Last Updated: October 13, 2025

⚠️ ZERO TOLERANCE POLICY

SMASHER has a strict zero-tolerance policy against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Any content, behavior, or activity involving minors will result in immediate account termination and reporting to law enforcement authorities.

1. AGE RESTRICTION

SMASHER is ONLY for users 18 years of age or older.

  • Users must provide their date of birth during registration
  • We verify that all users are at least 18 years old
  • Anyone under 18 is prohibited from using the service
  • We do not knowingly collect information from minors

2. PROHIBITED CONTENT & BEHAVIOR

The following are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate permanent ban:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM): Any images, videos, or content depicting minors in sexual situations
  • Grooming: Any attempt to establish contact with minors for sexual purposes
  • Solicitation: Requesting or sharing sexual content involving minors
  • Sexualization of Minors: Any content that sexualizes children or adolescents
  • Age Misrepresentation: Lying about age to access the platform
  • Facilitating Contact with Minors: Using the platform to connect with or find minors

3. DETECTION & PREVENTION

We employ multiple safeguards to prevent CSAE:

  • Age Verification: Mandatory birthdate collection and verification at registration
  • Content Monitoring: Review of reported content for CSAM
  • User Reports: Easy reporting mechanism for suspicious behavior
  • Automated Detection: Systems to flag suspicious accounts and content
  • Account Verification: Email verification required for all accounts

4. REPORTING MECHANISM

If you encounter any content or behavior involving minors:

  1. Report immediately using the in-app report button
  2. Block the user to prevent further contact
  3. Email us urgently: smashermain@gmail.com
  4. Contact authorities: Report to local law enforcement or NCMEC

Emergency Contact: smashermain@gmail.com

Response Time: Reports involving minors are reviewed immediately (within 1 hour)

5. RESPONSE PROTOCOL

When CSAE content or behavior is reported or detected:

  1. Immediate Action: Account is suspended immediately
  2. Content Preservation: Evidence is preserved for law enforcement
  3. Law Enforcement Notification: We report to appropriate authorities including:
    • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
    • Local law enforcement
    • FBI Cyber Division (if applicable)
  4. Permanent Ban: User is permanently banned from the platform
  5. Device/IP Ban: Device and IP address are blocked to prevent re-registration

6. COOPERATION WITH LAW ENFORCEMENT

We fully cooperate with law enforcement agencies:

  • We respond to all legal requests for information
  • We preserve evidence as required by law
  • We provide testimony and documentation when subpoenaed
  • We proactively report CSAM to NCMEC as required by law

7. NCMEC REPORTING

As required by U.S. law (18 U.S.C. § 2258A), we report all instances of apparent child sexual abuse material to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) CyberTipline.

NCMEC CyberTipline: www.cybertipline.org

8. USER RESPONSIBILITIES

All users must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Provide accurate age information
  • Report any suspicious content or behavior
  • Not attempt to contact minors through the platform
  • Not share or request content involving minors

9. CONSEQUENCES OF VIOLATIONS

Violations of this policy result in:

  • Immediate permanent ban from SMASHER
  • Report to law enforcement and NCMEC
  • Preservation of evidence for prosecution
  • Cooperation with authorities in investigation and prosecution
  • Potential criminal charges under federal and state law

10. LEGAL FRAMEWORK

This policy is designed to comply with:

  • 18 U.S.C. § 2258A (CSAM reporting requirements)
  • 18 U.S.C. § 2251 et seq. (Sexual exploitation of children)
  • PROTECT Act
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • State laws regarding child protection

11. INTERNATIONAL COOPERATION

We cooperate with international organizations:

  • INTERPOL

  • National and international law enforcement agencies
