Account & Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your SMASHER account and all associated data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the SMASHER app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Account
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm deletion

Processing time: Immediate

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion via email:

Send Deletion Request Email

Or manually email:

  • To: smashermain@gmail.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Body: Include your registered email address

Processing time: Within 48 hours

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently delete:

  • Your profile information (name, bio, birthdate)
  • All photos and media you uploaded
  • Your chat messages and conversations
  • Your location data
  • Your account credentials (email, password)
  • Your preferences and settings
  • Your match history
  • All other personal data

What We Retain

We may retain the following for legal or security purposes:

  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics (no personal identifiers)
  • Records required by law (e.g., age verification logs)
  • Data in backup systems (deleted within 30 days)
  • Records of policy violations or bans (to prevent re-registration)

Deletion Timeline

  • Immediately: Account is deactivated and inaccessible
  • Within 24 hours: Data deleted from active systems
  • Within 30 days: Data deleted from backup systems
⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will lose all your data, matches, and conversations. If you want to use SMASHER again, you will need to create a new account.

Alternative: Temporarily Deactivate

If you're not sure about permanent deletion, you can temporarily deactivate your account:

  1. Open the SMASHER app
  2. Go to Settings → Privacy
  3. Toggle "Hide My Profile"

This hides your profile from other users but keeps your data intact.

Data Export

Before deleting your account, you can request a copy of your data:

  • Email: smashermain@gmail.com
  • Subject: Data Export Request
  • Processing time: Within 7 days

We will send you a file containing all your personal data in a portable format.

Questions?

If you have questions about account deletion or data retention:

  • Email: smashermain@gmail.com
  • Response time: Within 48 hours

Your Rights

Under data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Export your data (data portability)
  • Object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at smashermain@gmail.com
