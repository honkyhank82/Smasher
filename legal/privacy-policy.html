Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 13, 2025

1. INTRODUCTION

SMASHER ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Personal Information:

  • Email address
  • Date of birth (for age verification)
  • Display name
  • Profile photos
  • Bio/description
  • Approximate location (for matching)

Automatically Collected:

  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Usage data
  • Log data

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Match you with nearby users
  • Send verification emails
  • Improve our services
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. INFORMATION SHARING

We do NOT sell your personal information.

We may share information with:

  • Service providers (hosting, email delivery)
  • Law enforcement (if legally required)
  • Other users (profile information only)

5. DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability

7. DATA RETENTION

We retain your data while your account is active. Upon account deletion, we delete your data within 30 days, except where required by law.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our service is only for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will immediately delete their account.

9. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare R2: For photo and media storage
  • Resend: For email delivery
  • Fly.io: For application hosting

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

10. LOCATION DATA

We collect approximate location data to show you nearby users. Your exact location is never shared with other users. You can control location permissions in your device settings.

11. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. CONTACT US

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at:

Email: smashermain@gmail.com

Response Time: We respond to all requests within 48 hours.

13. DATA DELETION

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Open the SMASHER app
  2. Go to Settings → Account
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

Or email us at smashermain@gmail.com with "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line.
