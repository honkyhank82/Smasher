Community Guidelines

SMASHER is a community for respectful connections. Follow these guidelines to keep our community safe and welcoming.

BE RESPECTFUL

  • Treat others as you want to be treated
  • No harassment, bullying, or hate speech
  • Respect boundaries and consent
  • Accept rejection gracefully
  • Be kind in your messages

BE AUTHENTIC

  • Use real, recent photos of yourself
  • Don't impersonate others
  • Be honest in your profile
  • Don't catfish or deceive others
  • One account per person

BE SAFE

  • Meet in public places for first meetings
  • Tell someone where you're going
  • Trust your instincts
  • Don't share personal information too quickly
  • Report suspicious behavior immediately
  • Never send money to people you meet on the app

BE APPROPRIATE

  • No explicit content as profile pictures
  • Keep conversations respectful
  • No illegal content
  • No spam or solicitation
  • No commercial activity without permission

PROHIBITED BEHAVIOR

The following will result in immediate account termination:

  • Harassment or threats
  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Content involving minors
  • Non-consensual intimate images
  • Illegal activity
  • Scams or fraud
  • Violence or self-harm
  • Impersonation

ENFORCEMENT

Violation Consequences:

  • First offense: Warning
  • Second offense: Temporary suspension (7 days)
  • Third offense: Permanent ban

Serious violations (illegal content, threats, harassment of minors) result in immediate permanent ban and may be reported to law enforcement.

REPORTING

If you see violations or feel unsafe:

  1. Use the in-app report feature on the user's profile
  2. Block the user to prevent further contact
  3. Email smashermain@gmail.com for urgent issues
  4. Contact local authorities if you're in immediate danger

We review all reports within 24 hours.

WHAT WE DON'T TOLERATE

  • Harassment: Repeated unwanted contact, threats, or intimidation
  • Hate Speech: Content attacking people based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, etc.
  • Spam: Unsolicited commercial messages or repetitive content
  • Scams: Attempts to defraud or deceive other users
  • Fake Profiles: Using someone else's photos or false information
  • Minors: Any content involving people under 18

CONTENT GUIDELINES

Profile Photos:

  • Must clearly show your face
  • Must be recent (within 2 years)
  • No group photos as main photo
  • No explicit nudity
  • No copyrighted images (celebrities, cartoons, etc.)

Gallery Photos:

  • Must be of you
  • No illegal content
  • No graphic violence
  • No hate symbols

Messages:

  • Be respectful and appropriate
  • No unsolicited explicit content
  • No harassment or threats
  • No spam or commercial solicitation

APPEALS

If you believe your account was suspended or banned in error:

  1. Email smashermain@gmail.com
  2. Include your registered email address
  3. Explain why you believe the action was incorrect
  4. We will review within 5 business days

UPDATES

We may update these guidelines from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of updated guidelines.

QUESTIONS?

Contact us at smashermain@gmail.com

Remember: SMASHER is a community. Your actions affect others. Be the kind of person you'd want to meet on this app.
