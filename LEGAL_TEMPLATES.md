## Data Deletion Instructions

```
DATA DELETION

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data.

TO DELETE YOUR ACCOUNT:

Method 1: In-App
1. Open SMASHER app
2. Go to Settings
3. Tap "Account"
4. Tap "Delete Account"
5. Confirm deletion

Method 2: Email
1. Send email to privacy@smasherapp.com
2. Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
3. Include your registered email address
4. We will process within 48 hours

WHAT GETS DELETED:
- Your profile information
- All photos and media
- Chat messages
- Location data
- Account credentials

WHAT WE RETAIN:
- Aggregated, anonymized analytics
- Records required by law (e.g., age verification)
- Backup data (deleted within 30 days)

TIMELINE:
- Immediate: Account deactivated
- 24 hours: Data deleted from active systems
- 30 days: Data deleted from backups

After deletion, your account cannot be recovered.
```
